Nikah

Marriage (nikah) is a solemn and sacred social contract between bride and groom.

Mahr

Mahr is a token commitment of the husband's responsibility and may be paid in cash, property or movable objects to the bride herself.

Sermon

The assembly of nikah is addressed with a marriage sermon (khutba-tun-nikah) by the Muslim officiating the marriage.

Walima

After the consummation of the marriage, the groom holds a banquet called a walima. The relatives, neighbors, and friends are invited in order to make them aware of the marriage.
